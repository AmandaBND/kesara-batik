## Technical changes

- Rewrote homepage title and description around search intent.
- Added natural, visible batik/bathik buying content and FAQs to the homepage.
- Added clean category URLs: `/women`, `/men`, `/kids`, `/family-kits`, `/accessories`, `/new-arrivals`.
- Added unique category titles, descriptions and keyword themes.
- Added Organization, OnlineStore, WebSite, CollectionPage, BreadcrumbList, FAQPage and Product JSON-LD.
- Added product offers, price currency, availability and aggregate rating schema.
- Added route-level canonical URLs, social metadata and crawler directives.
- Added a generated XML sitemap that includes active product URLs during Vercel builds.
- Removed policy pages from the sitemap.
- Added `noindex` through both meta tags and Vercel `X-Robots-Tag` headers for the privacy, refund and terms pages.
- Added a 1200×630 social preview image.
- Updated internal navigation to use clean canonical category URLs.

## After deployment

1. Open Google Search Console for `https://www.kesarabathik.com`.
2. Submit `https://www.kesarabathik.com/sitemap.xml`.
3. Inspect the homepage and request indexing.
4. Request indexing for `/products`, `/women`, `/men`, `/kids`, `/family-kits`, `/accessories`, `/new-arrivals`, and important product pages.
5. Use Search Console Removals for the three policy URLs only if they must disappear from results quickly. Their permanent `noindex` will remove them after recrawling.
6. Connect Google Merchant Center and submit products for free listings.
7. Create or verify the Google Business Profile and ensure the website, business name, phone and address are consistent.

Google does not use the meta keywords tag for ranking. The tag remains as a supplementary hint for other systems, while the actual keyword work is implemented in visible content, headings, internal links, metadata and structured data.
